Our Services

Root Canal Treatment

Treatment for infection of the pulp when decay reaches the third layer of the tooth.

Crown and Bridges

Dental caps and replacement teeth designed to match your natural smile strength.

Post and Core

Restoration for teeth with inadequate tissue to retain a conventional crown.

Orthodontics (Braces)

Straightening and aligning irregular teeth for better chewing and aesthetics.

Dental Implants

The ideal solution for replacing missing teeth with natural-looking results.

Oral Surgery

Surgical procedures for tooth removal, jaw repositioning, or tumor removal.

Scaling & Gum Treatment

Removing plaque and tartar to control the severity of gum diseases.

Dental Restoration

Repairing missing or decayed teeth using fillings, crowns, or dentures.

Emergency Dentistry

Professional treatment for urgent issues involving pain or tissue damage.

Bleaching

Removing stains and restoring natural tooth color through whitening.

Smile Designing

Cosmetic procedures considering size, color, and shape for a perfect smile.

Full Mouth Rehab

Combining restorative treatments to rebuild and fortify your smile.

Pediatric Dentistry

Early-stage dental care and hygiene routines for your baby’s teeth.

Complete & Partial Dentures

Removable artificial teeth for normal functioning and profile enhancement.
